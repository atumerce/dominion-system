# Dominion Methodology

> **Version:** 1.0  
> **Classification:** Public

---

## 1. The Trinity

Dominion operates as an integrated trio. No module functions in isolation.

| Module | Question It Answers | Output |
|--------|---------------------|--------|
| **Balance** | *Which way is the structure biased?* | Direction (Bull / Bear / Conflict) |
| **Anchor** | *Where is the high-probability engagement zone?* | Zone boundaries (Upper / Lower / Liquidity) |
| **Trigger** | *Is there a valid pattern at that zone right now?* | Signal (Pattern + Confluence Score) |

A valid setup requires alignment across all three. If Balance reads Conflict, or Anchor finds no zone, Trigger suppresses all output.

---

## 2. Balance — Direction

Multi-timeframe price-action synthesis without oscillators.

- **HTF Lead Wave:** Dominant trend structure via algorithmic swing detection
- **Core BOS:** Break-of-Structure confirmation to validate or invalidate the HTF read
- **LTF Momentum:** Raw candle-body impulse to time entry within the structural window

Chaos Radar: real-time ATR ratio and spread monitoring. If volatility spikes beyond calibrated thresholds, the engine outputs "Chaos" — pause and reassess.

---

## 3. Anchor — Location

Momentum-validated zone detection. Not support/resistance drawn by hand. Zones derived from volume-anchored impulse analysis.

- **Reversal Entry:** Level where price has historically reversed in the operator's favour
- **Breakout Entry:** Level where structure break confirms continuation
- **Final Target:** Level where the move is statistically likely to exhaust
- **Liquidity Zone:** Level that has survived multiple tests without breaking

Strategic Lock: high-confluence zones are locked until price validates or invalidates them.

---

## 4. Trigger — Timing

Pattern recognition only within zones validated by Anchor and aligned with Balance direction.

**Reversal:** Sovereign Trap (pinbar sweep), Momentum Engulfing

**Continuation:** Rocket Gap (FVG breakout), Inertia Flag (inside-bar + breakout)

No pattern is valid if the zone is unconfirmed or the timeframe is in Chaos.

---

## 5. What Dominion Does NOT Do

| Claim | Reality |
|-------|---------|
| Predict exact price path | No. It recognises patterns at zones. |
| Trade automatically | No. It prepares setups. You decide. |
| Eliminate risk | No. It improves precision. Market risk remains. |
| Replace your judgement | Absolutely not. |
